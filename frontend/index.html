About Pet Pal

At Pet Pal adoption center, it is our passion to find loving homes for pets in need. Since our organization was founded, we have only promoted adoption, and we firmly believe that every pet has a right to a peaceful and secure future. Our goal is to build enduring relationships that have a positive effect on adoptive families as well as pets. One adoption at a time, Ethio Pet Adoption is committed to changing lives via openness, kindness, and steadfast support. Come along with us as we create a community where each paw has a lifetime home.
